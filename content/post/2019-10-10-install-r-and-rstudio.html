1. Download R

Download R from this site (I choose the first URL in US, just for convenience, technically they are all the same): https://cran.cnr.berkeley.edu

Download based on your system: Windows/IOS/Linux

Then install.

2. Download RStudio

From here: https://rstudio.com/products/rstudio/download/

choose the first one, free.

Then install.

Now you are ready to go!
